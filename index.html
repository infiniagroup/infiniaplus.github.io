FAQs

Why not just hire a full-time staff?

It takes time and money. Even with the cost of a full-time staff, outside resources are often needed.Our services can be primary, or supplemental.

Is there a limit to how many requests I can have?

The only limit is the number of active requests.

How fast will I receive the work product?

Design work, within 48 hours, and for strategy and consulting work, within 72 hours.

Who is doing the work?

Our team and our network of specialists.

What programs do you design in?

Illustrator, InDesign, and others

How do I request work?

Through Trello and other project management software.

What if I don't like the work product?

We provide a satisfaction guarantee. We work to create a work productcrafted to your specifications.

How long of a commitment is a subscription?

12 months.
